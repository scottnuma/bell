iOS

  • Open Bell in safari
  • Tap the square icon with the arrow point up in the bottom navigation bar
  • Tap the plus icon to add to homescreen

Android

  • Open Bell
  • Tap the menu icon in the top right
  • Scroll down and click "Add to homescreen"
  • Click "Add"

Updates

iOS will in most cases update Bell. Android does not automatically update. To force an update on either, do the following.

  • Delete the app
  • Reinstall
